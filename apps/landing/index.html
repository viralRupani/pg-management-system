Questions, answered

Everything you're wondering about

Is there a setup fee?
None at all. There's no onboarding charge and no contract. You start free for 30 days, and after that you only pay ₹10 per occupied bed each month — nothing for empty beds.
Do I need a payment gateway?
No. Residents pay you directly over UPI and simply upload a screenshot in the app. You approve or reject it in one tap. Because the money never routes through us, there's no gateway cut on your rent.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Every PG's data is strictly isolated using bank-grade multi-tenant separation, encrypted in transit and at rest. No other owner — and no other PG — can ever see your residents, payments or documents.
Can residents use it on iPhone and Android?
Both. The resident app works on any iPhone or Android phone, and managers can run the dashboard from a phone or laptop browser — no installation required.
Can I manage more than one property?
Absolutely. Manage anywhere from 1 to 10+ properties from a single login, switch between them instantly, and give each manager their own access.
